Our monthly stacks were created and tested by professional athletes—triathletes, marathon runners, rowers, cyclists, swimmers, and more. Our roster of athletes tracked their sustained energy levels, muscle fatigue, and recovery to hone in on a pack that not only could optimize their performance but could be as convenient and smooth as possible; these guys hate feeling like robots!
